About us

A Nigerian Based Charity Organization

With more than half of Nigeria's population in poverty, paying medical bills has become a herculean task.

We are a team of college students who help patients overcome this obstacle of healthcare unaffordability in Nigeria by raising funds for them. We seek to ensure that the sick won't have to go through arduous and depressing financial troubles to pay their medical bills.

Our Mission

Why We Do What We Do

  • The Problem
  • Our Motivation
  • Your Impact

One of the many problems in the Nigerian Healthcare system is affordability. Millions of Nigerians are not capable of funding their complete treatment bills both in private hospitals and even in public healthcare centers, where treatment costs are usually less.

Some of these patients are then denied treatment or held back in the hospital after treatment. In cases where it is clear that the quality of healthcare to be received is high, the problem of affordability becomes a significant and saddening obstacle for the patients.

Donate Now

We are a team of college students who help patients overcome this obstacle and contribute to solving this problem of healthcare unaffordability in Nigeria by raising funds for them. We seek to ensure that the sick won't have to go through arduous and depressing financial troubles to pay their medical bills.

We believe that one life, every life, is worth a fortune; instead of letting patients deteriorate in health or get crippled financially, psychologically, and otherwise because they cannot afford treatment, we cater to their medical bills through this initiative.

Donate Now

We cannot do this alone. We need you, your funds, and your support to enable financially constrained patients across Nigeria to AffordHealth!
